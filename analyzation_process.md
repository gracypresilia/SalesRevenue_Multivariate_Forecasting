### Columns Explanation
- **Date:**
- - `date`: 1096 unique dates from 1st January 2022 to 31st December 2024.
**Store Data**

- **store_id:** 10 unique store IDs across region and city.
- **store_type:** 3 unique store types for each unique store ID.
- **region:** 4 unique regions for each unique store ID.
- **city:** 10 unique regions for each unique store ID.
- **store_area_sqft:** Various store area for each unique store ID.
- **product_id:** 50 unique product IDs.
- **category:** 5 unique category for each unique product ID.
- **base_price:** Base price for each unique product ID.
- **final_price:** Various final price for each product in each unique conditions (date, store, etc.).
- **discount_pct:** Discount percentages ranging from 0-30% for each product in each unique conditions.
- **promotion:** Whether the product is on promotion or not (1 or 0) for each product in each unique conditions.
- **is_holiday:** Whether the day is holiday or not (1 or 0).
- **day_of_week:** Day of the week ranging from 0-6 (Monday-Sunday).
- **weekend:** Whether the day is weekend or not (1 or 0).
- **units_sold:** Number of units sold for each product in each unique conditions.
- **returns:** Number of units returned by customer for each product in each unique conditions.
- **net_units:** Netto of units sold for each product in each unique conditions.
- **revenue:** Gross revenue for each product in each unique conditions.
- **net_revenue:** Netto revenue for each product in each unique conditions.
- **avg_rating:** Customers rating for each product in each unique conditions.
- **online:** Whether the product is sold online or offline (1 or 0).
